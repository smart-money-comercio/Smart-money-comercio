Smart Money AI Business Plan

Executive Summary

Smart Money AI is an AI-powered investment intelligence platform that combines congressional trading activity, insider transactions, institutional investment signals, defense industry intelligence, cybersecurity trends, and artificial intelligence analysis to identify emerging investment opportunities.

The platform is designed to provide investors with actionable intelligence through daily reports, Telegram alerts, email briefings, and portfolio recommendations.

Unlike traditional investment research tools, Smart Money AI focuses on identifying capital flows into high-growth sectors including artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, autonomous systems, drone warfare, counter-drone technologies, and defense modernization.
