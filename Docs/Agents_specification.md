AI Agent Specifications

Agent 1: Smart Money Analyst

Purpose

Identify stocks receiving significant attention from smart money sources.

Inputs

- Congressional trades
- Insider purchases
- Earnings reports
- Institutional activity

Outputs

- Smart Money Score
- Investment thesis
- Risk rating
- Watchlist ranking

---

Agent 2: Defense Intelligence Analyst

Purpose

Identify defense, cybersecurity, AI warfare, and drone-related opportunities.

Inputs

- DoD contracts
- NATO contracts
- Defense news
- Contract awards
- Insider activity

Outputs

- Defense Intelligence Score
- Contract significance rating
- Strategic outlook
- Alert recommendations

---

Agent 3: Market Intelligence Analyst

Purpose

Monitor broad market conditions.

Inputs

- Market indices
- Sector performance
- Interest rates
- Economic indicators

Outputs

- Market outlook
- Sector rankings
- Risk assessment

---

Agent 4: Portfolio Manager

Purpose

Combine all agent outputs into portfolio recommendations.

Inputs

- Smart Money Scores
- Defense Scores
- Market Scores

Outputs

- Portfolio allocation
- Buy candidates
- Watchlist updates
- Rebalancing recommendations
